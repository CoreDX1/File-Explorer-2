Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers

How much storage do I have?

By default, each user gets 50GB of storage space. Premium users get 200GB, and Business users get 2TB. You can upgrade your storage plan at any time from the Storage section of your account.

Can I recover deleted files?

Yes, deleted files are kept in the Recycle Bin for 30 days before permanent deletion. You can restore them at any time during this period. Files in the Recycle Bin still count toward your storage limit.

How secure is my data?

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. All files are encrypted with AES-256 encryption. We also offer two-factor authentication for additional account security.

Can I share files with people outside the organization?

Yes, you can share files with external users. You can control the permissions (view, edit, comment) and set expiration dates for shared links. You can also require external users to sign in or set a password for the shared link.
